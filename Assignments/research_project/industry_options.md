## **Research & Academia**
- **Sub-Category / Examples**
  - Industry Research Labs (Google Research, Meta FAIR, Microsoft Research, NVIDIA Research)
  - Top-Tier Universities (MIT CSAIL, Stanford AI Lab, CMU, Berkeley BAIR)
  - Hybrid Roles (Allen Institute for AI, Vector Institute, Mila)
- **2025–2027 Growth Outlook & Key Trends**
  - Industry labs expanding (but selective); academic hiring remains tight
  - Focus on foundational models, interpretability, alignment research
  - Growing trend of research scientists with joint academic appointments
- **Salary Range (US, mid-senior level)**
  - Industry Research: $180K–$350K (competitive with engineering roles at same companies)
  - Academia (tenure-track): $120K–$200K (+ grants, consulting)
  - Postdocs/Research Scientists (non-tenure): $80K–$150K
- **Why It's Attractive / Risks**
  - Publication freedom, intellectual exploration, mentorship opportunities
  - Lower TC than pure industry (especially academia), slower career progression
  - Academic jobs extremely competitive; "publish or perish" pressure

## **Speculative / High-Volatility** 
- **Sub-Category / Examples**
  - **Web3 / Crypto** (Coinbase, Kraken, Solana Labs, Alchemy)
    - Still recovering from 2022–23 crash; many firms struggling or pivoting
    - Regulatory uncertainty (SEC enforcement, global compliance)
  - **Autonomous Vehicles** (Waymo, Aurora, Zoox, Tesla FSD)
    - Consolidation ongoing (Cruise shutdown, layoffs across sector)
    - Long path to profitability; heavy capital requirements
  - **Early-Stage Robotics** (Figure AI, 1X Technologies, Agility Robotics)
    - Exciting tech, but unproven product-market fit
    - Dependent on hardware breakthroughs + manufacturing scale
- **2025–2027 Growth Outlook & Key Trends**
  - Crypto: Modest recovery if regulatory clarity improves; Bitcoin ETF approvals helping sentiment
  - AV: Slow, capital-intensive scaling; Waymo leads but expansion cautious
  - Robotics: Hype cycle peak; many startups will fail, but 1-2 breakout winners possible
- **Salary Range (US, mid-senior level)**
  - $150K–$400K+ (extreme equity variance—paper gains often evaporate)
- **Why It's Attractive / Risks**
  - **Upside:** If the company moons, life-changing equity (10–100x)
  - **Downside:** Boom-or-bust; high layoff risk, equity may be worthless
  - Best for those with financial cushion or high risk tolerance
